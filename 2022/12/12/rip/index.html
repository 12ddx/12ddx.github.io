    RIP配置命令

    • 创建RIP进程并进入RIP视图

      [h3c] rip [process-id]

    • 在指定网段接口上使能RIP

      [h3c-rip-1] network ip

    • 配置接口工作在抑制状态

      [h3c-rip-1] silent-interface [interface-number | all]

    • 使能RIP水平分割功能

      [h3c-Ethernet1/0] rip split-horizon

    • 使能RIP毒性逆转功能

      [h3c-Ethernet1/0] rip poison-reverse
